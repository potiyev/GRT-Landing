F1-inspired reaction training with multiple game modes, global leaderboards, and 10 official team themes. Free on iOS.

Privacy Policy

Four Ways to Play

🏜

Launch Sequence

Five lights count down, then go dark. Tap the instant they go out. Millisecond-precision timing with F1 driver comparisons after every round.

▦

Grid Trainer

Tap highlighted cells on 3x3, 4x4, or 5x5 grids. Play untimed for accuracy or race the clock in 10-second timed mode.

👥

Two-Player Split Screen

Face off on one device. Both players tap Ready, lights count down, and the fastest reaction wins. Instant rematch support.

🌐

AR Mode

Place a 3D launch gantry in your room with augmented reality. Pinch to resize, tap to react. Full particle effects on iOS 18+.
