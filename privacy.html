Privacy Policy for Grid Reaction Timer

Last Updated: August 14, 2025

What Information We Collect

We don’t collect personal information directly from you. However, because the app now includes Google AdMob ads, Google and its advertising partners may collect certain device and usage data to display ads, prevent spam, detect abuse, and help ensure fair play.

Information That Stays on Your Device

  • Your Game Scores: All your reaction times are saved only on your device. We never see them.
  • Your Settings: Things like your chosen theme, sound preferences, and vibration settings are stored only on your device.
  • App Icon Choice: Your selected app icon is remembered on your device only.

Apple Game Center (Optional)

  • If you choose to use Game Center, Apple handles your leaderboard scores according to their privacy policy.
  • You can play the game without Game Center — it’s completely optional.
  • Game Center lets you compete with other players, but we don't see your personal information.

Guest Mode

  • When you enable Guest Mode, the app temporarily stores scores that disappear when you turn off Guest Mode.
  • This lets someone else use your device without affecting your personal scores.

Advertising & Third-Party Tracking

This app uses Google AdMob to display full-screen interstitial ads.

What AdMob may collect

  • Device identifiers (such as Apple’s IDFA)
  • Coarse location (estimated from your IP address)
  • App interaction data (such as ad views and taps)
  • Performance and crash data

How this information may be used

  • Serving and personalizing ads
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Preventing spam and fraudulent activity
  • Ensuring fair play within the app and ad ecosystem

Data collected by AdMob is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can control whether tracking occurs by adjusting your iOS App Tracking Transparency and Limit Ad Tracking settings. If you decline tracking, ads may still appear but will be less targeted.

What We Don't Do

  • We don’t directly collect personal information such as your name or email.
  • We don’t sell your information.
  • We don’t run our own analytics or tracking systems — all tracking is done by Google AdMob and its partners.

Optional Tip Purchases

The app includes optional tip buttons to support development:

  • These are processed through Apple’s App Store.
  • We don’t store any payment information.
  • Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy.

Children’s Privacy

While this app is safe for children in its gameplay, AdMob ads may not always be child-directed. If your child is using the app, we recommend enabling device parental controls and limiting ad tracking in your iOS settings.

Your Control

You have control over your data and experience:

  • Delete the app to remove all your scores and settings.
  • Use Guest Mode to play without affecting your personal scores.
  • Choose whether to use Game Center or not.
  • Manage ad tracking permissions in your iOS privacy settings.

App Permissions

The app may request:

  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback during gameplay (you can turn this off in settings).
  • Game Center: To save scores to leaderboards (completely optional).
  • Tracking Permission: Only if you allow AdMob to use your device’s advertising identifier for personalized ads.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app works with your data, we’ll update this policy. Since we don’t collect your contact information, check back here occasionally if you’re curious about updates.

Contact

This app is developed by a hobbyist developer. If you have questions about privacy, you can reach out through the App Store.
