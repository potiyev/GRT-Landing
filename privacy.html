Privacy Policy for Grid Reaction Timer

Last Updated: August 17, 2025

The Simple Version

We made this game app fun and safe! We show ads to keep the app free, but we don't spy on you or collect your personal information.

What We DON'T Take From You

  • We don't ask for your name, email, or phone number
  • We don't look at your photos or contacts
  • We don't track where you go or what other apps you use
  • We don't record you or listen to your conversations
  • We don't know your exact location

What Stays Just On Your Phone

Your Game Stuff

  • Your Scores: All your reaction times stay on your phone. We never see them.
  • Your Settings: Things like colors, sounds, and vibration choices stay on your phone.
  • Your App Icon: The app icon you pick stays on your phone.

Game Center (If You Want)

  • You can choose to connect to Apple's Game Center to compete with friends
  • This is totally optional - you can play without it
  • Apple handles this, not us

Guest Mode

  • When someone else plays on your phone, their scores disappear when they're done
  • Your real scores stay safe

About The Ads

We show ads to keep the app free. Google helps us show these ads.

What Google Might See

  • What kind of phone you have
  • Roughly what city you're in (not your exact address)
  • If you tap on ads
  • If the app crashes

Why: To show ads, see if they work, and stop bad people from cheating.

Important: The ads are the same for everyone - they're not special just for you.

How We Keep Things Safe

  • We don't save your personal stuff on our computers
  • Google handles ad information safely

For Kids and Parents

This app is safe for kids to play. We don't collect personal information from children.

Tip Jar (Optional)

You can give us tips to help make the app better:

  • Apple handles all the money stuff
  • We never see your payment information
  • It's totally optional - the app works great without tips!

What You Can Control

  • Delete the app anytime to remove all your stuff
  • Use Guest Mode when friends want to play
  • Choose if you want Game Center or not
  • Change ad settings in your phone's privacy settings

What The App Might Ask For

  • Vibration: To make your phone buzz when you play (you can turn this off)
  • Game Center: To save scores and compete with friends (optional)

If You Want To Learn More

Google has their own rules about ads and privacy:

  • Google's Ad Rules
  • Google's Privacy Rules

If We Change Something

If we update this privacy stuff, we'll put the new version here. Check back once in a while if you're curious!

Questions?

Have questions about privacy? Visit https://gridreactiontimer.com to contact us!
